## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the boundaries of acceptable conduct on the Sizzle platform. We provide powerful compute resources; with that power comes responsibility.

## General Principles

1. **Be Legal:** Don't use Sizzle for anything illegal.
2. **Be Ethical:** Don't create harm for others.
3. **Be Honest:** Don't misrepresent yourself or your usage.
4. **Be Respectful:** Don't abuse the platform or other users.

## ‚ùå PROHIBITED USES

### 1. Illegal Content & Activities

You MAY NOT use Sizzle for:

- **Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM):** Absolutely prohibited. Zero tolerance. Reported to authorities.
- **Non-consensual intimate imagery:** Including deepfakes of real people without consent
- **Terrorism-related content:** Recruitment, planning, propaganda
- **Illegal weapons:** Instructions for creating weapons of mass destruction
- **Drug trafficking:** Manufacturing, distribution of illegal substances
- **Fraud:** Scams, phishing, identity theft
- **Hacking:** Unauthorized access to systems, data theft

### 2. Harmful AI Applications

You MAY NOT use Sizzle for:

- **Deepfake generation of real people** without their explicit consent
- **Disinformation campaigns:** AI-generated fake news, election interference
- **Harassment bots:** Automated systems to harass individuals
- **Discriminatory systems:** AI that discriminates based on protected characteristics
- **Surveillance systems** for authoritarian purposes
- **Autonomous weapons:** AI systems designed to harm humans

### 3. Infrastructure Abuse

You MAY NOT use Sizzle for:

- **Cryptocurrency mining** without explicit written permission
- **DDoS attacks** or participation in botnets
- **Spam generation** at scale
- **Web scraping** that violates target sites' ToS
- **Circumventing** our security or rate limits
- **Reselling** our services without authorization

### 4. Resource Abuse

You MAY NOT:

- Run sessions indefinitely without actual usage (idle resource hoarding)
- Attempt to exploit billing systems
- Create multiple accounts to circumvent limits
- Share account credentials
