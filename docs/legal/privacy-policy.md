# Sizzle — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [DATE]
**Last Updated:** [DATE]

## Our Commitment

At Sizzle, privacy is a core value. We built this platform because we believe you should control your data and your compute. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

**Key Promise:** We do NOT use your data to train AI models. Your creations are yours.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Account Info** (email, name) | Account creation, communication | Until account deletion |
| **Payment Info** (card details) | Billing (via Stripe) | Handled by Stripe |
| **Phone Number** (optional) | 2FA, higher tier verification | Until account deletion |
| **Support Requests** | Customer service | 3 years |

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Usage Logs** | Service delivery, debugging | 90 days |
| **Session Metadata** | Billing, analytics | 1 year |
| **IP Address** | Security, fraud prevention | 90 days |
| **Device Info** | Compatibility, support | 90 days |

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Content of your Pump Sessions (we don't see what you generate)
- ❌ Model weights you train
- ❌ Files in your Pump VPN storage (encrypted at rest)
- ❌ Inference requests/responses through your API

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide the Service** — Create sessions, process payments, deliver compute
2. **Communicate** — Send receipts, updates, support responses
3. **Improve** — Analyze usage patterns to make the platform better
4. **Secure** — Detect fraud, abuse, and security threats
5. **Comply** — Meet legal obligations when required

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Train AI models on your content
- Share data except as described here

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

### 3.1 Service Providers

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Stripe** | Payment processing | Payment info |
| **AWS/GCP/Cloudflare** | Infrastructure | Encrypted data |
| **GPU Providers** | Session delivery | Session metadata only |

All providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

### 3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by:
- Valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Law enforcement with proper authorization
- Protection of rights, safety, or property

We will notify you of legal requests unless prohibited by law.

### 3.3 Business Transfers

If Sizzle is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

---

## 4. Data Security

We protect your data with:

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
- **Access Control:** Role-based, principle of least privilege
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and alerting
- **Audits:** Regular security assessments
- **Isolation:** Your sessions run in isolated environments

No system is 100% secure. We promptly notify affected users of any breach.

---

## 5. Your Rights

### 5.1 All Users

You can:
- **Access** your data via account settings
- **Update** your information anytime
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data in standard formats

### 5.2 EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

Additional rights under GDPR:
- **Right to be forgotten** — Request deletion
- **Right to portability** — Export in machine-readable format
- **Right to restrict processing** — Limit how we use your data
- **Right to object** — Object to certain processing
- **Right to withdraw consent** — Anytime, without penalty

**Data Controller:** Sizzle, Inc.
**Contact:** privacy@sizzle.io

### 5.3 California Users (CCPA)

You have the right to:
- Know what data we collect
- Request deletion
- Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights
